Get to Know Me

My journey in the world of wine began more than 10 years ago when I discovered my passion as a bartender. Over the years, I have held various roles within the wine industry, from working at a vineyard in Portugal to creating wine courses. My true joy, however, has always been hosting wine tastings. Despite the challenges, including being forced to leave my country due to the Russian invasion, my passion for wine has remained unwavering. Join me on an exciting wine journey that brings flavors and stories from around the world directly to your glass.

With over a decade of experience in the wine industry, I have gained a deep understanding of wines and their unique characteristics. My goal is to share this knowledge and passion with you, making wine accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Let's explore the world of wine together and discover hidden gems along the way.
